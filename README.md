## 🎯 Program Overview

Forward Deployed AI Engineers (FDEs) act as tech-consulting chameleons. They possess deep technical mastery of Generative AI infrastructure while maintaining sharp business acumen to deploy bespoke AI solutions directly inside client ecosystems. 

*   **Credential:** PG Certificate from CEC, IIT Roorkee
*   **Status:** IIT Roorkee Alumni Status (CEC)
*   **Immersive Learning:** 3 days of live campus immersion at IIT Roorkee
*   **Delivery:** Live interactive sessions by IIT Faculty and Futurense Industry Champions

## 🗺️ Curriculum Blueprint & Core Modules

### 🧠 Module 1: FDE Mindset & Problem Structuring
* **FDE Paradigms & Role:** Embracing the forward-deployed engineering methodology.
* **Systems Thinking:** Designing scalable, interconnected AI architectures.
* **Business → Tech Translation:** Mapping client requirements to technical specs.
* **Problem Decomposition:** Breaking down complex business problems into solvable tasks.

### 🔀 Module 2: Data & Pipeline Thinking
* **ETL & Pipeline Basics:** Building robust data ingestion and processing systems.
* **Data Quality & Monitoring:** Ensuring high-fidelity data feeds for reliable AI outputs.

### 🪄 Module 3: Prompt Engineering & LLM Workflows
* **Prompt Engineering Fundamentals:** Mastering basic token and context optimization.
* **Prompt Structuring:** Designing structural, template-driven prompts.
* **Advanced Prompting & LLM Integration:** Chaining models into automated enterprise workflows.

### 🔄 Module 4: ML Foundations & Model Lifecycle
* **ML Fundamentals:** Core statistical models and mathematical principles.
* **Model Training & Evaluation:** Assessing model performance with validation metrics.
* **Experiment Tracking:** Logging and versioning models with modern tooling.
* **ML Pipelines & Deployment:** Automating delivery from local environments to production.

### 🧬 Module 5: Deep Learning and NLP
* **Neural Networks:** Core architectures, backpropagation, and activation functions.
* **CNNs & Vision:** Building vision-based processing pipelines.
* **Transfer Learning:** Fine-tuning pre-trained weights for specialized tasks.
* **NLP Fundamentals:** Tokenization, word vectors, and syntactic parsing.
* **LLM-based NLP Systems:** Adapting large language models for domain-specific language tasks.
* **DL Recap & Integration:** Synthesizing deep learning concepts into cohesive systems.

### 🚀 Module 6: Prototyping, Deployment & Consulting
* **MVP & AI App Prototyping:** Rapid building of lightweight, functional AI frontends.
* **Client Discovery & Scoping:** Auditing enterprise environments and uncovering client friction.
* **PoC → MVP → Deployment:** Engineering the transition from sandbox to production.
* **Demo & Storytelling:** Communicating technical value to cross-functional stakeholders.
